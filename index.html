Bio-Digital Twin Simulator

Clinical Problem Clinical simulation platforms are either low-fidelity or prohibitively expensive. Nurses and educators need high-fidelity, on-demand patient simulation that produces emergent clinical behavior — not scripted scenarios.

A full-stack physiological simulation engine where clinical cascades emerge from isolated agent interactions rather than hard-coded scripts. Built on an async multi-agent architecture with real-time waveform rendering.

  • Multi-agent physiological engine (Python, PyTorch, asyncio)
  • 4-channel real-time waveform rendering (ECG, arterial, pleth, capno)
  • Emergent clinical cascades — not scripted behavior
  • GPU-accelerated batch computation for population-scale studies
  • React/TypeScript frontend with WebSocket bridge
  • Evidence-based pharmacokinetics and clinical scoring
